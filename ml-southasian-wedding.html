Questions

Common Questions

Can you cover both Hindu and Muslim ceremonies? +

Yes — we have extensive experience with both Hindu multi-day celebrations and Muslim ceremonies including Mehndi, Nikah, Rukhsati, and Walima. We approach each ceremony with cultural understanding and sensitivity.

Do you cover multi-day weddings? +

Absolutely — this is our specialty. South Asian weddings often span 3–5 days. Our packages are built around multi-day coverage with consistent photographers across all events so your gallery tells one unified story.

How do you handle dark banquet halls and evening ceremonies? +

We're experts in low-light and artificial lighting. Using portable LED lighting systems and professional flash setups, we ensure every image is cinematic regardless of the venue's lighting conditions.

Can we book individual events (just Mehendi, just Sangeet)? +

Yes. Standalone event coverage is available starting at $1,200. Many families book us for individual events first, then upgrade to full multi-day packages when they see the results.

Do you work with specific venues in NJ/NY for South Asian weddings? +

We're familiar with the top South Asian wedding venues across New Jersey and New York — including Edison, Parsippany, Iselin, and venues in Queens and Long Island. We know the lighting challenges and best angles in most of them.
