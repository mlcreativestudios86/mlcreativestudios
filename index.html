Questions

You Asked,
We Answered

How far in advance should we book? +

Most couples book 9–12 months in advance, especially for peak season (May–October). If your date is sooner, reach out — we can often accommodate.

Do you travel outside of New Jersey? +

Absolutely. We regularly cover weddings across New York, Connecticut, and Pennsylvania. Destination weddings are available — contact us for travel rates.

How many photos will we receive? +

Depending on your package, you'll receive between 300 and 700+ fully edited, high-resolution images delivered via private online gallery.

What is your editing style? +

Cinematic and timeless — warm, film-inspired tones with rich contrast. We avoid heavy filters that date quickly. Your photos will look just as beautiful in 20 years.

What happens if you get sick on our wedding day? +

We maintain a trusted network of professional photographers who can step in. Your wedding will always be covered — this is in every contract.
